FAQ

Clear answers for a still-early project.

Microwave Stove is still early-stage, so the goal here is clarity, not hype.

Q1. What is Microwave Stove?

+

A hybrid cooking concept that combines microwave-based heating principles with a stove-like user experience.

Q2. Is this a finished commercial product?

+

No. It is an early-stage prototype and still under development.

Q3. How much power does the prototype use?

+

The current prototype has been demonstrated at around ~800W.

Q4. What makes it different from a normal microwave oven?

+

It is designed as an open-format cooking concept with stove-like usability rather than a closed microwave cavity experience.

Q5. Why is this relevant?

+

It has potential for lower power cooking, solar-compatible use cases, and future clean-cooking applications.

Q6. Is this public and non-confidential?

+

Yes. The website contains only high-level public-safe information.
